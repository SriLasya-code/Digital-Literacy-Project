STAY SAFE ONLINE PREVENTION CHECKLIST For College Students in India

GENERAL SAFETY
 1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account never reuse them
 2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
 3. Never click on links received via unknown SMS, WhatsApp or email
 4. Always check the URL before entering login details look for https://
 5. Do not use public WiFi for any sensitive activity like banking or email
 6. Regularly update your apps and phone software for security patches

UPI & FINANCIAL SAFETY 
 7. Remember  a UPI collect request means MONEY LEAVES your account. Never enter your PIN to "receive" money.
 8. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV or bank account details with anyone not even someone claiming to be from your bank.
 9. Keep a separate low-balance account for online transactions and only transfer money when needed.
 10. Always verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before sending money fraudsters use IDs that look almost identical to real ones.

IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG 
 11. Report cybercrime immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 12. Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (free, available 24x7)
 13. Inform your bank immediately to freeze the transaction if possible
 14. Do not panic or pay any "recovery fee" that is another scam
